3D Printing

A current hobby and interest of mine is 3D Printing. I have a multidisciplinary background in robotics, software, and hardware engineering and with my interest in artwork, this hobby was a no brainer! I currently own a Creality CR-10S, Monoprice Mini Delta and have been fortunate enough to work with other printers such as the Ultimaker 3+. Feel free to clone my Marlin-1.1.9_CR-10s firmware GitHub repo as I try to maintain this and keep it as up to date as possible. I would also love to share some of my latest creations and collections on Thingiverse as well. Happy Printing!
